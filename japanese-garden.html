Visiting Japanese Garden

  • Date: Sunday, May 28
  • Time: 12:00-1:00 pm ET
  • Where: Como Japanese Garden
  • Cost: $8.00
RSVP

As the weather begins to warm up and the cherry blossoms start to bloom, it is the perfect time to visit the beautiful Japanese garden nearby. The Japanese garden is a place where we can appreciate the art of nature and immerse ourselves in the tranquility of traditional Japanese culture. We would like to invite all members who are interested to join us for a group visit to the Japanese garden. This will be a great opportunity for us to bond as a community and enjoy the beauty of the garden together.

The visit is open to all members of the Japanese Association, regardless of age or background. We welcome those who are new to the Association as well as those who have been with us for a while.

The details of the visit are as follows:

Date: Sunday, May 28
Time: 12:00-1:00 pm ET
Location: Como Japanese Garden
If you would like to join us, please RSVP by Friday, May 26.

We look forward to seeing you all there!
