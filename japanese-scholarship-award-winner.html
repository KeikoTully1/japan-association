2023 The Japanese Scholarship Award Winner

  • Name: Jessica Norman
  • Occupation: Student - freshman
  • School: Star State University
  • Location: St. Paul, Minnesota


"I am writing to express my heartfelt gratitude for the Japanese scholarship that I have been awarded. It is an honor to receive such a prestigious scholarship and I am deeply thankful to the Japan Association for giving me this opportunity.

The scholarship will not only help me financially but will also allow me to pursue my dream of studying in Japan. The support and encouragement from the Japan Association has made me feel confident in my abilities and has given me the motivation to work hard towards achieving my goals.

I am also grateful for the sponsor's generosity and kindness in supporting my studies. Their contribution has made a significant impact on my academic journey and I am truly appreciative of their support.

Thank you again to the Japan Association and the sponsor for making this scholarship possible. I promise to make the most of this opportunity and strive to become a valuable member of society."
