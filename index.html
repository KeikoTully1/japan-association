Today's Recipe: Cardamom Rice

Cardamom rice is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish that is popular in many parts of the world. It's made with fragrant cardamom pods, basmati rice, and a few other simple ingredients. The dish is easy to prepare and can be served as a side dish or as a main course.

    So, let's get started. Here's what you'll need:

  1. 1 cup basmati rice
  2. 2 cups water
  3. 1 tablespoon butter or ghee
  4. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  5. 3 cardamom pods, crushed
  6. 2 cloves
  7. 1 cinnamon stick

Now, I know what you're thinking: how on earth can you make rice in a hotel room? Well, it's easier than you might think. All you need is a small saucepan and a hot plate. Most hotel rooms have these items, but if you can't find them, you can always ask the front desk to bring them to your room.

First things first: rinse the rice in a fine mesh strainer and soak it in water for 30 minutes. This will help to remove any excess starch and make the rice fluffier. Drain the rice and set it aside.

In a medium-sized saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the cardamom pods, cloves, and cinnamon stick and saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. This will help to infuse the butter with the spices and give the rice a delicious aroma.

Next, add the rice to the pan and stir to coat it with the butter and spices. Add the water and salt and bring it to a boil. Once it's boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and simmer for 18-20 minutes until the rice is tender and the water has been absorbed.

Now, at this point, you can serve the rice as is, or you can get creative and add some extra ingredients to jazz it up. Personally, I like to add a handful of toasted almonds and some chopped fresh parsley. The almonds add a nice crunch and the parsley adds a bit of color and freshness.

So there you have it, folks: cardamom rice. It's a simple but tasty dish that's perfect for those times when you're stuck in a hotel room and craving some home-cooked food. And the best part is, you can customize it to your liking by adding different spices, nuts, or herbs.

Cardamom Rice Recipe

Cook Time: 20 minutes
Prep Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon butter or ghee
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cardamom pods, crushed
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice in a fine mesh strainer and soak it in water for 30 minutes. Drain the rice and set aside.
  2. In a medium-sized saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the cardamom pods, cloves, and cinnamon stick and saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the rice to the pan and stir to coat it with the butter and spices. Add the water and salt and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and simmer for 18-20 minutes until the rice is cooked and the water has been absorbed.
  5. Remove the pan from the heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve.
